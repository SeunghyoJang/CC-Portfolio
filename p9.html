Project#9 Consentful Interface

code link

Description

Since the camera is used, it started with an intro seeking consent before access. It is to give the user the right to choose. Pressing the Enter allows access to the camera, allowing you to use a photo filter, and pressing Q denies access to the camera. According to the Fries model, Freely given: Giving the user a free choice before starting. Reversible: If you change your mind while using it in the middle or don't want to use it, you can always quit by pressing the quit button. Informed: In the initial intro, this camera filter will not be turned on without consent and will not be recorded or used elsewhere. Enthusiastic: Even though people are not interested, they do not necessarily use this camera filter to force themselves to access something. Specific: Explain in as much detail as possible what seeks consent to allow access to the camera filter.
