Project#2 Face Generator

Description

I drew a scared face character. If you move the mouse out, the face of the character will get bigger and if you put it inside, it will become smaller. As the face grows, the distance between the eyes becomes farther away, and as the face becomes smaller, the gap between the eyes becomes closer. Both cheeks have freckles that continue to wiggle, and the mouth also moves constantly on both sides. By clicking the mouse, you can see that the color of the face continues to change randomly in any sizes.
